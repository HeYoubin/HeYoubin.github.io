Recent Work

This page summarizes recent engineering and research directions in a public-safe form. It is intended to show the technical scope of my work without exposing client information, internal system details, or non-public products.

Due to confidentiality requirements, this page only describes generalized technical workflows and personal contributions. Internal code, datasets, system implementation details, screenshots, and operational product examples are not publicly displayed.

Recent Engineering and Research Directions

Polar-orbiting Satellite Fire Detection and Preprocessing

I have worked on preprocessing and sample-preparation workflows for polar-orbiting satellite fire detection. The public-safe description includes fire-point matching, feature extraction, positive / negative sample construction, and preparation for machine-learning-based fire detection experiments.

Operational Remote Sensing Product Generation

Recent engineering work has involved automated generation of remote sensing products, time-series compositing, raster statistics, thematic mapping, and structured outputs for downstream operational systems.

Crop Growth and Yield Estimation Workflows

I have contributed to crop growth assessment and county-level yield estimation workflows using satellite-derived vegetation indicators, meteorological variables, crop distribution information, county boundaries, and statistical yield records.

Explainable GeoAI for Wildfire Risk Prediction

In parallel with engineering work, my research direction focuses on explainable GeoAI for wildfire risk prediction, including machine learning, spatial heterogeneity, SHAP-based interpretation, and GeoShapley-based regional mechanism analysis.

Technical Documentation and System Design

I also contribute to technical documentation, workflow summaries, testing notes, and system-design communication that translate remote sensing algorithms into reproducible and maintainable engineering processes.
